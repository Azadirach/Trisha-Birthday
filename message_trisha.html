Happy Birthday, you weirdo!

Here we are at Chapter 23—a whole new beginning for someone who somehow manages to be loud, brilliant, and perpetually sleepy all at once. It's quite the combination, really.

You're the kind of person who lights up every room you walk into (even if you'd rather be napping in it). Your rambunctious energy is infectious, your intelligence is genuinely inspiring, and your optimism—even when you're running on what can only be described as fumes—reminds everyone around you that life is meant to be lived fully and joyfully.

Twenty-three looks good on you. Not that you needed any help in that department—you've always been pretty, inside and out. But there's something about this chapter that feels right, like all those late nights (and early mornings you slept through) were leading somewhere wonderful.

So here's to you: to new adventures, to more laughter that's probably too loud for the setting, to naps that are well-deserved, and to that beautiful, chaotic, brilliant way you move through the world. May this year bring you everything you hope for and a few delightful surprises along the way.

Keep being exactly who you are—weird, wonderful, and absolutely unforgettable.
