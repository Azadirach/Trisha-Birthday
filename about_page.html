How this was made

I started working on this sometime in november because medicine was driving me crazy and I need to keep creating something to have a palliative effect on my mood.

The hardest part of the whole thing was getting the games to fit on small screens - I knew you'd see this on a phone. Responsive design is hard with games like this because the keyboard has to render at an adequate size.

Other than that it was pretty much smooth sailing. As I write this, I'm putting together the finishing touches before the thing goes live. I hope you like it :)
